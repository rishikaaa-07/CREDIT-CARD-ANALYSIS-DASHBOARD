An interactive Power BI dashboard that provides a detailed analysis of customer transactions segmented by demographics, income, and behavior. This report helps businesses understand customer patterns and optimize strategies based on revenue, satisfaction, and engagement metrics.

## Features
- KPI cards for:
  - Total Revenue (55.32M)
  - Transaction Count (656K)
  - Customer Satisfaction Score (3.19)
  - Total Income (576M)
- Filters by quarter and customer category (Silver, Blue, Gold, Platinum)
- Line chart: Revenue by week segmented by gender
- Bar charts:
  - Revenue by age group
  - Revenue by dependent count
  - Revenue by marital status
  - Revenue by education level
  - Revenue by income group
- Pie chart: Revenue by top 5 states
- Customer segment comparison (Male vs Female)
- Tabular breakdown by customer job, revenue, interest earned, and income
  ### 3. Credit Card Transaction Report Dashboard
- KPI cards for revenue, transaction count, transaction amount, and total interest
- Filters by card category and usage type
- Visuals:
  - Revenue by chip usage (swipe, chip, online)
  - Revenue by education level, expenditure type, customer job
  - Revenue by card category
  - Quarterly revenue and transaction trends
  - Tabular breakdown by card category, transaction amount, and interest earned

## Technologies Used
- SQL (PostgreSQL) for data extraction and transformation
- Power BI for dashboard design and interactivity
- DAX for calculated metrics and dynamic filtering
